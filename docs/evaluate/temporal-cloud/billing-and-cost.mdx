As an Account Owner, you can view billing and cost details at any time. 

The typical billing cycle begins on the first of the month (UTC). Your first month is prorated based on when you sign up. 

You have the ability to download your Invoices by clicking the download icon by the date. 

## About the Billing page

The **Billing** page includes the following information:

- Your current balance
- Recent bill
- Invoice history
- (If applicable) Credit Grants
- (If applicable) Credit Purchase
- (If applicable) Credit Usage
- (If applicable) Credits Remaining

If you are not on a standard plan, the Billing page may not include all of the information stated above. 

## How to navigate to the Billing page

To navigate to your invoices, go to **Settings > Billing**

## Current balance

The current balance card displays the balance accrued thus far in the billing cycle. You will see an updated date of when this data is updated. Note, this information will change based on usage. This data also corresponds to the first line in the Invoices table. 

## Recent bill

The recent bill card display the last bill amount. If you are paying invoices through the Stripe portal, you will see a **Pay Now** button. The **Pay Now** button will take you to the Stripe portal for you to complete your payment. If the account set up for auto-payment, no action is required to pay the bill manually but it’s available if needed. 

## Invoice table

For the current date or billing period, you will not have the download invoice option available as the Invoice has not been finalized. 

This Invoices table has the following information: 

- Date (UTC) - date range for the invoice
- Type - Invoice type, credit purchase or cloud usage
- Credits Purchase - Amount of credits added to your account
- Credits Purchase Amount - Amount paid for credits purchase
- Credits Usage - Amount of credits used this billing cycle
- Subtotal - Invoice amount
- Total - Balance remaining after Credits Applied
