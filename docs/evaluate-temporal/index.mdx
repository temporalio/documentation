## Temporal

Temporal is an open-source platform designed to simplify the development of distributed applications.
It enables developers to build reliable systems faster, while making your software fault-tolerant by default.
Thousands of companies of all sizes are leveraging Temporal's capabilities for both mission critical and standard workloads.
